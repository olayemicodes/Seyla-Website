About Us

SEYLA PREGCARE is a digital product that connects pregnant patients
with doctors online regardless of location for twenty-four hours.

Our Mission

We at SEYLA PREGCARE are here to support you throughout your
pregnancy experience. We give you prompt responses to your
inquires and wellness advice.

Our Vision

To ensure a pregnancy experience unfazed by anxiety, and a hitch-free delivery.
